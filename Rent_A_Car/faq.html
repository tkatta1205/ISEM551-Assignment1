What insurance is included in your rental price?

Full insurance package is included in our rental price - Third party liability, collision damage waiver and theft and fire protection. This prepaid insurance is compulsory and gives you peace of mind.


What is car refundable security deposit?

The refundable car security deposit covers unlikely events that are not coevered by insurance - lost of car keys and car returned with empty tank, lost car equipment etc.


Is the mileage restricted on your car rentals?

Yes car mileage is UNLIMITED.


Where do I pick-up/drop-off the car once booked?

You can collect your car rental from the nearest location. For available locations go to locations we serve on this website.


What happens if my flight / bus/train is delayed or cancelled?

Please inform us as soon as possible so we can reschedule your car collection accordingly to new arrival time.
